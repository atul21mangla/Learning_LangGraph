<!-- SECTION 1: GENERATIVE AI -->
<div style="padding: 20px; border: 1px solid #444; border-radius: 10px; background-color: #ecd6d6; color: #333;">
  <h2 style="color:#ff6600; border-bottom: 2px solid #ff6600; padding-bottom: 5px;">🌈 What is <span>Generative AI</span>?</h2>
  <p style="font-size: 1.1em; line-height: 1.6;">
    <b>Generative AI (GenAI)</b> is the creative powerhouse of the artificial intelligence world. It focuses on <b>content synthesis</b>—using advanced machine learning models (like LLMs or Diffusion models) to analyze patterns in massive datasets and generate entirely 
    <span style="color:#ff00cc; font-weight: bold;">new, original content</span>.
  </p>
  <ul>
    <li>🎨 <b>Outputs:</b> High-quality text, photorealistic images, code, music, and video.</li>
    <li>🧠 <b>Mechanism:</b> Predicts the "next best" token or pixel based on probability.</li>
    <li>💬 <b>Interaction:</b> Primarily <i>reactive</i>—it provides a response based on a specific prompt.</li>
  </ul>
</div>

<br>

<!-- SECTION 2: AGENTIC AI -->
<div style="padding: 20px; border: 1px solid #444; border-radius: 10px; background-color: #ecd6d6; color: #333;">
  <h2 style="color:#ff00cc; border-bottom: 2px solid #ff00cc; padding-bottom: 5px;"><u>Agentic AI</u>: The "Doer" 🤖</h2>
  <p style="font-size: 1.1em; line-height: 1.6;">
    <b>Agentic AI</b> represents a paradigm shift from "AI you talk to" to "AI that works for you." It is a <b>proactive system</b> designed to fulfill complex goals by breaking them down into actionable steps and executing them autonomously.
  </p>
  
  <h3 style="color: #008080;">🔄 The Agentic Workflow (The Loop)</h3>
  <ol style="line-height: 1.8;">
    <li>📝 <b>Planning:</b> The AI decomposes a high-level goal (e.g., "Research and book a flight") into a sequence of logical sub-tasks.</li>
    <li>⚡ <b>Action:</b> It interacts with the real world using <b>Tools</b> (APIs, web browsers, databases) to execute those tasks.</li>
    <li>👀 <b>Observation & Reflection:</b> It analyzes the outcome of its actions. If a tool fails or the environment changes, it <b>adapts</b> its strategy in real-time.</li>
  </ol>

  <h3 style="color: #008080;">Key Characteristics:</h3>
  <div style="display: grid; grid-template-columns: 1fr 1fr; gap: 10px; font-weight: 500;">
    <span>🤖 <b>Autonomous:</b> Minimal human intervention.</span>
    <span>🎯 <b>Goal Oriented:</b> Focuses on outcomes, not just replies.</span>
    <span>🧠 <b>Reasoning:</b> Uses logic to solve multi-step problems.</span>
    <span>🔄 <b>Adaptability:</b> Corrects its own errors.</span>
    <span>🌐 <b>Context Awareness:</b> Remembers past steps and constraints.</span>
    <span>🛠️ <b>Tool-Use:</b> Can "hands-on" operate software.</span>
  </div>
</div>

<br>

<!-- NEW SECTION: THE SECRET SAUCE (REASONING & MEMORY) -->
<div style="padding: 20px; border: 2px solid #00e6e6; border-radius: 10px; background-color: #001a1a; color: white;">
  <h2 style="color:#00e6e6;">🧠 Why Agentic AI is Different (Advanced Concepts)</h2>
  
  <h3 style="color: #ffcc00;">1. The ReAct Pattern (Reason + Act)</h3>
  <p>Unlike standard GenAI which "guesses" the answer, Agentic AI follows a <b>Thought → Action → Observation</b> loop. It thinks about what to do, does it, looks at the result, and then thinks again. This prevents "hallucinations" because the AI verifies its work at every step.</p>

  <h3 style="color: #ffcc00;">2. Short-term & Long-term Memory</h3>
  <p>While GenAI forgets the conversation after a while, Agentic AI uses <b>Vector Databases</b> as long-term memory to remember user preferences, past mistakes, and successful strategies from weeks ago.</p>

  <h3 style="color: #ffcc00;">3. Multi-Agent Systems (MAS)</h3>
  <p>The most powerful Agentic AI isn't just one agent. It's a <b>team of agents</b> (e.g., a "Coder Agent," a "Reviewer Agent," and a "Manager Agent") talking to each other to complete a massive project. </p>

<!-- SECTION 4: AUTONOMY & CONTROLS -->
<div align="center">
  <h1 style="color:#ff00cc; text-shadow: 1px 1px #333;">🦾 Mastering Autonomy 🦾</h1>
</div>

<blockquote style="font-style: italic; border-left: 5px solid #00e6e6; padding: 10px 20px; background: #f0f0f0; color: #333;">
  <b>Autonomy</b> is the degree of freedom an AI has to make decisions and execute actions without human hand-holding. While powerful, high autonomy requires <b>Safety Guardrails</b>.
</blockquote>

<h3 style="color: #df8b8b;">How we control Agentic Autonomy:</h3>
<ul style="line-height: 1.8;">
  <li>🔒 <b>Permission Scope:</b> Defining strict boundaries (e.g., <i>"The agent can read my calendar but cannot delete events"</i>).</li>
  <li>🧑‍⚖️ <b>Human-in-The-Loop (HITL):</b> Strategic checkpoints where the AI must pause and ask: <i>"I am about to spend $500, do you approve?"</i></li>
  <li>⏸️ <b>Override Controls:</b> A "Kill Switch" or manual intervention capability to stop an agent if it enters an infinite loop or behaves unexpectedly.</li>
  <li>🛡️ <b>Guardrails & Policies:</b> Hard-coded ethical and operational rules that the AI's reasoning engine cannot bypass.</li>
</ul>
